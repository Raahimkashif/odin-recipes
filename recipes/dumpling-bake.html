Dumpling Bake

Description

This dumpling bake uses frozen gyoza, baked in a garlicky coconut curry sauce with fresh spinach. It's very easy to assemble, and most of the ingredients can be kept in your pantry or freezer, ready to go. Serve over white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 handfuls fresh spinach
  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1/2 (11 ounce) bottle Thai red curry sauce, such as Trader Joe's®
  • 3 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic, or more to taste
  • 14 to 15 pieces frozen gyoza
  • crunchy chili oil, for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Stir coconut milk, red curry sauce, teriyaki sauce, and garlic together in a 9x13-inch baking pan. Scatter spinach evenly on top.
  3. Arrange gyoza in the baking dish. Use as many as can fit in the dish; up to about 15. Cover with foil.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through, about 35 minutes.
  5. Drizzle with chili crunch before serving.
